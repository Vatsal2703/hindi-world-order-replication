Addendum to the EMILLE Corpus manual

 

October 2004

 

This version of the EMILLE Corpus has been updated from the original release of the corpus in the second half of 2003.

 

A number of encoding glitches have been identified and repaired. We would like to thank the users of the Corpus for helping us to spot these.

 

In particular, we would like to acknowledge the efforts of Mike Maxwell and colleagues at the LDC, without whose help we could not have identified the encoding bugs that have been removed from the current version of the corpus.

 

Our thanks also go to Richard Xiao of Lancaster University, who validated (and amended many errors in) the SGML encoding of the corpus.

 

We would also like to thank ELRA for agreeing to take on distribution of the EMILLE Corpus.

 

 

Tony McEnery and Andrew Hardie

29th October 2004
